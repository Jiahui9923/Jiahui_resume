    Here are my projects & my life

    I like to do some projects and participate in some competitions. Here are some of my experiences.

    Books Management System

    My project is mainly to design the library management system, not responsible for the implementation of specific codes. The main content is to update and improve the efficiency of the school library management system. The main applications are for students and teachers. The system is not public. The design content is the reclassification of the book and the update of the database. There are three databases for professors, students and administrators. In addition, integrate all book content and design a new index to help students search better.

    Snake game

    Snake game is completed by using Python, a project jointly completed by me and my classmates. The one I designed was the snake variable. This includes designing variables such as length, speed and how to control the snake.
